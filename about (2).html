About PageMood

Built for readers who believe their books deserve a home as beautiful as the worlds inside them.

✦
✦ Our Story

PageMood was born from a simple idea — that tracking your reading life should feel as magical as the books themselves. Most book trackers feel like spreadsheets. We wanted something that felt like stepping into an enchanted library.

We built PageMood for the BookTok community, for the dark academia lovers, the fantasy readers, the cottagecore souls, the people who cry at fictional deaths and feel genuinely betrayed by plot twists. For readers who don't just consume books — they live inside them.

Every detail in PageMood was designed with intention — the flickering candles, the aged leather spines, the ambient fireplace sounds, the golden dust drifting through the air. We wanted your library to feel like a place you actually want to spend time in.

PageMood is completely free. No subscriptions, no hidden fees, no tricks. Just a beautiful home for your books.
